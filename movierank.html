Top 3 Favorite Movies.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Based on a novella by Stephen King, this film tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man wrongfully convicted of murder, and his unlikely friendship with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding. Set in Shawshank prison, the film explores themes of hope, redemption, and the human spirit, as Andy plans a daring escape while changing the lives of those around him. It's known for its emotional depth and powerful performances.
